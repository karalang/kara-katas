Prism

A local image workbench. Drop a photo — it's processed entirely in your browser, and never uploaded. The pixel math (grayscale, bilinear & Lanczos-3 resize so far) runs in Kāra compiled to WebAssembly; the browser only decodes and re-encodes the file.

Drop an image here, or click to choose
JPEG · PNG · WebP — stays on your device
🔒

Where's the upload? There isn't one — open the Network tab and watch it stay empty. The image is decoded to pixels by the browser, handed to a Kāra process(op, sw, sh, dw, dh) export that runs the selected kernel, and blitted back to the canvas. Download re-encodes the canvas locally.
