Prism

A local image workbench. Drop a photo — it's processed entirely in your browser, and never uploaded. The pixel math runs in Kāra compiled to WebAssembly; the browser only decodes and re-encodes the file.

Drop an image here, or click to choose
JPEG · PNG · WebP — stays on your device

Where's the upload? There isn't one — open the Network tab and watch it stay empty. The image is decoded to pixels by the browser, handed to a Kāra process(w, h) export that runs the grayscale kernel, and blitted back to the canvas. Download re-encodes the canvas locally.
